# Project Overview

## Title

Data-driven analysis of the role of transcription factors in hematopoietic stem cell differentiation into six progenitor compartments

## Background

This repository contains my contribution to a collaborative graduate bioinformatics project completed in 2017. The project integrated DNA methylation and RNA-seq data to investigate transcription factors involved in early human hematopoietic differentiation.

The complete project was performed by four team members, each contributing different analytical components. My primary responsibilities included transcription factor motif enrichment analysis using HOMER, RNA-seq analysis, downstream transcription factor expression analysis, and integration of motif enrichment results with normal hematopoietic and leukemia RNA-seq datasets.

## Biological Question

The objective was to identify transcription factors that are active during differentiation of hematopoietic stem and progenitor cells by integrating:

- DNA methylation profiles
- RNA-seq gene expression
- transcription factor motif enrichment
- leukemia expression datasets

The hypothesis was that hypomethylated regulatory regions contain enriched transcription factor binding motifs associated with lineage-specific gene expression.

## Data

The project used publicly available datasets from the BLUEPRINT Epigenome Project.

Main datasets included:

- Whole genome bisulfite sequencing (DNA methylation)
- RNA-seq from normal hematopoietic progenitor populations
- RNA-seq from AML samples
- RNA-seq from CLL samples

The normal populations included:

- HSC
- MPP
- CMP
- GMP
- MEP
- CLP
- MLP

## Overall Workflow

The complete team workflow consisted of:

1. DNA methylation preprocessing and differential methylation analysis
2. RNA-seq preprocessing and differential expression analysis
3. Integration of methylation and expression results
4. HOMER transcription factor motif enrichment
5. Transcription factor expression analysis
6. Leukemia validation using AML and CLL RNA-seq data

## My Contributions

I primarily contributed to:

- RNA-seq processing and differential expression analysis

- Transcription factor clustering analyses
- Preparation of figures for the final poster

The repository also contains documentation describing the complete project workflow for reproducibility and context, while clearly distinguishing my individual contributions from the collaborative work.
