# Hematopoietic Transcription Factor Regulatory Analysis

## Overview

This repository contains an integrative bioinformatics analysis investigating transcription factor regulation during human hematopoietic stem and progenitor cell differentiation.

The project combines genome-wide DNA methylation and RNA sequencing data from the BluePrint Epigenome Project to identify lineage-specific regulatory regions, characterize transcription factor binding motif enrichment, and compare transcription factor signatures between normal hematopoietic differentiation and leukemia.

This work was completed as part of a collaborative graduate bioinformatics research project.

---

## Biological Question

Which transcription factors are associated with lineage-specific differentiation of human hematopoietic stem and progenitor cells, and are these regulatory programs reflected in leukemia?

---

## Data Sources

- BluePrint Epigenome Project
- Whole-genome bisulfite sequencing (DNA methylation)
- RNA-seq from normal hematopoietic progenitor populations
- RNA-seq from acute myeloid leukemia (AML) and chronic lymphocytic leukemia (CLL)

---

## Analysis Pipeline

1. DNA methylation analysis
2. Identification of hypomethylated promoter and enhancer regions
3. RNA-seq differential expression analysis
4. Integration of methylation and gene expression results
5. Transcription factor motif enrichment using HOMER
6. Comparison of transcription factor signatures in leukemia
